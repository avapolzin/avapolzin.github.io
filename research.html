Research

Current Work

Dwarf galaxies (van Dokkum Group), Yale University

Observational, extragalactic research focused on dwarf galaxies.

21cm cosmology (Newburgh Group), Yale University

Working on the Canadian Hydrogen Intensity Mapping Experiment (CHIME) pipeline. Formerly looked into using a drone to calibrate the Hydrogen Intensity and Real-time Analysis eXperiment (HIRAX).

Past Research

X-ray transients and variables (Margutti Group), Northwestern University

Generating a complete picture of the X-ray Duration-Luminosity Phase Space (DLPS) for transient and variable phenomena. Using real light curves where data are available.

Eclipsing binary yield of the Vera Rubin Observatory (Geller Group), Northwestern University

Simulating eclipsing binary observations over the ten-year run of the VRO's Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST) to determine the observed fraction, as well, as the fraction for which we'll be able to recover the physical properties.

Micro-X (Figueroa Group), Northwestern University

X-ray telescope payload for launched Summer 2018. Intended as possible means of dark matter direct detection.

Nealson Lab, University of Southern California

Meant as preparation for anticipated summer work at the Jet Propulsion Lab, Pasadena, CA. Environmental geomicrobiology with astrobiological implications.
