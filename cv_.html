Research Experience

Graduate Research Assistant (Newburgh Group), Yale University Department of Physics, June 2020 - Present

Working on the Canadian Hydrogen Intensity Mapping Experiment (CHIME) pipeline.

Graduate Research Assistant (van Dokkum Group), Yale University Department of Astronomy, August 2019 - Present

Supervisors: Dr. Pieter van Dokkum

Observational extragalactic astrophysics focused on dwarf galaxies.

Research Assistant (Margutti Group), Center for Interdisciplinary Exploration and Research in Astrophysics (CIERA), Northwestern University, January 2019 - July 2019

Supervisors: Dr. Raffaella Margutti

Worked on a review of X-ray transients in the duration-luminosity phase space.

Intern, Vera Rubin Observatory (formerly Large Synoptic Survey Telescope), Center for Interdisciplinary Exploration and Research in Astrophysics (CIERA), Northwestern University, June 2017 - July 2019

Supervisors: Dr. Aaron Geller and Dr. Adam Miller

Used python code (based in the packages ellc and gatspy) with a full-scale galactic simulation to determine a new minimum prediction for the Legacy Survey of Space and Time's eclipsing binary yield over its ten-year run. This research is fleshed out by determining the factors that impact binary detection and looking further at the idea of accurate identification of both binaries and their periods.
